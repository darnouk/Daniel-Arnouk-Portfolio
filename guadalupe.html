Guadalupe Mountains Contour Map

Below is a topographical map that delineates the varied and steep elevation gradients within the Guadalupe Mountains of El Paso, TX. Crafting this map was a journey unto itself.



Steps to Create This Map:

  1. Source data from NASA's Shuttle Radar Topography Mission.
  2. Use the statistics function in ArcGIS Pro to generalize and smooth out some of the terrain layers.
  3. Employ the Contour tool within the Spatial Analyst toolbox to generate a feature class of contours.
  4. After considerable time spent on cartographic design, finalize the representation to visualize the area's topography.

In a time when topographical maps are often overloaded with data and compromise on visual appeal, aim for a distinctive approach. Maps should not only be informative but also visually compelling, inviting the viewer to immerse in the narrative they weave. In this instance, the map hints at the rigorous trek that thousands complete each year in this beloved region of Texas.
