Broadband Disparity in Western Wisconsin

Below is an infographic I made while learning about broadband data and access and its role within Wisconsin, which is where I live.



This infographic focuses on two cities in particular, New Richmond and Avery, WI, due to their proximity to the MN border and to the Twin Cities. The purpose of this map is to show how an area considered relatively close to a major metropolitan hub can still struggle having their internet accessibility needs met.
