Dane County Upload vs. Download Speed Distribution

Below is a swipe map I created through a multi-step process.



I first gathered the data for upload and download speeds from the Public Service Commission (PSC) of Wisconsin's website.
Due to the massive size of the data and rendering time, I decided to focus on one county, and what better county to focus on than the location of UW-Madison!
I then polished my data in ArcGIS Pro, uploaded it to ArcGIS Online, and then used Experience Builder for the first time to create this swipe map.
To get the map to load properly, you may be prompted to allow pop-ups on this site. Hit "allow" to load the map.
Due to the massive number of polygons needed to load this map even when narrowing the area of focus to Dane County, the map may take a couple seconds to load based on your connection and device's processing power.
